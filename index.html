How it works

Simple setup.
Powerful results.

Sirat uses GPS to calculate your precise prayer times using established calculation methods.
02

Pick which apps get blocked during prayer. Instagram, TikTok, games — your choice, enforced by iOS.
03
Pray undistracted
When a prayer arrives, the lock activates automatically. No willpower needed.
